About Fine Industial Corporation 11111

M/s .Fine Industries Corporation (FIC) was formed in the year 1992 by well experienced Mr. Ahtisab Khan to provide reliable and quality service to the Marine, Petroleum plants, Oil solvent plants, Water Treatment plants, Fertilizers Plant and Mechanical Heavy Industries. We are an ISO 9001:2008 certified company. Our organization is a team of expertise professionals comprising of qualified Engineers, Technical & Supervisory staff with diverse Technological excellence to impart the best services as per client requirement and to International standards. During the past 16 years we have been achieving our objectives of complete client satisfaction with the dedicated and devoted services. We arrange for door delivery to the customers if they need.

We are in manufacturing and supplies of all types of Flanges and Fittings used in the Offshore projects, Ship Repairs, Fabrication involving Mechanical Heavy Industries, Petroleum Plants, Oil Solvent Plants, Structural, Piping with proper test certificates.
