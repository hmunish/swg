Welcome To Steel World Group

Manufacturers, Exporters And Suppliers of Steel And Hardware Products

For more than a quarter-century, Steel World Group has been a beacon of reliability and excellence in the realm of steel and building materials throughout Malawi. Our deep-rooted presence across the nation enables us to intimately understand and cater to the diverse requirements of our Malawian clientele. From robust pipes to versatile plates, sturdy fencing to essential hardware supplies, we furnish top-tier products alongside unmatched service, empowering construction endeavors of every scale. With a steadfast dedication to quality and a relentless pursuit of customer satisfaction, Steel World Group stands as the steadfast ally for all your building essentials in Malawi. Steel World Group has proudly served Malawi's construction needs with top-notch steel, hardware, and building materials. With a nationwide network, we understand the unique challenges faced by Malawian builders and homeowners, offering tailored solutions and unparalleled service to drive success in every project.
